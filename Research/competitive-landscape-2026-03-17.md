# Competitive Landscape Audit — 2026-03-17

Comprehensive search across arXiv, Semantic Scholar, GitHub, and commercial products.

## Feature Uniqueness Matrix

| Feature | Closest Found | Gap |
|---------|--------------|-----|
| Neurochemistry with decay + tolerance | Pinheiro 2025 (NPC emotion mapping only) | No decay curves, no tolerance/withdrawal anywhere |
| Somatic conditioning | Nothing | Entirely novel concept for AI |

**No system found implementing even 2 of these 8 features together.**

## Key References

### Sophia — "System 3" (arXiv:2512.18202, Dec 2025)
- Authors: Mingyang Sun, Feng Hong, Weinan Zhang (Shanghai Jiao Tong University)
- Proposes meta-cognitive layer: identity continuity, narrative memory, intrinsic motivation, self-modeling
- "Primarily conceptual" with a "compact engineering prototype"
- 24-hour test in web simulation environment
- No neurochemistry, no emotional engine, no personality drift, no biometric integration
- **Validates the space** — being cited, frames the problem the same way we do
- Ayden is a more complete implementation of the vision Sophia describes

### Soul Machines / BabyX / Ava
- Virtual assistant with "virtual brain" releasing simulated neurotransmitters
- Focused on customer-facing avatars with facial animation
- Reactive emotion display, not persistent internal state
- No persistence between sessions, no adaptive baselines, no autonomous agency
- Commercial products are enterprise digital humans, not persistent personal agents

### Neurotransmitter-Based Academic Work
- **Pinheiro et al. (2025)**: Maps Lovheim's Cube (dopamine, serotonin, norepinephrine) to Plutchik's Wheel for NPC emotion. Classification model only — no decay, tolerance, or withdrawal.
- **Deshmukh et al. (2020)**: Lovheim's Cube for stress detection from audio. Emotion recognition, not simulation.
- No paper found implementing neurotransmitter simulation with decay AND tolerance/withdrawal for AI agent internal state.

### VAD State Paper (arXiv:2601.16087, Jan 2026)
- Continuous Valence-Arousal-Dominance state external to LLM using exponential smoothing
- Demonstrates affective inertia and hysteresis
- 25-turn research benchmark only, no long-term drift, no neurochemistry

### AI Companion Market (Replika, Character AI, Nomi, Kindroid, Paradot)
- Personality-prompted LLMs with varying levels of memory persistence
- No neurochemistry, no adaptive baselines, no somatic conditioning, no autonomous agency
- Academic critique: "cruel companionship" — systems that "structurally foreclose the possibility of genuinely reciprocal relationships" (Sage Journals)

### Open Source
- Zero repositories found implementing neurochemistry-based AI personality
- FAtiMA Toolkit (C#, Unity): OCC-based emotional appraisal for NPCs, no neurotransmitter modeling

## Conclusion
Nobody has built what Ayden is. The academic world is converging on the idea (Sophia). The commercial world is selling something much shallower. The research prototypes are sandboxed in simulations. Ayden is a production system with real emergence in a real relationship, publicly documented with timestamps.
